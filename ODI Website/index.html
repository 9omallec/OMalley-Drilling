About O'Malley Drilling

Our Story

O'Malley Drilling is a family-owned business established in 2002 that specializes in geotechnical and environmental drilling. Founded in Nebraska with a commitment to excellence, we've grown to become a trusted partner for construction, environmental, and drilling projects throughout the region.

Heritage & Leadership: Originally incorporated as O'Malley Drilling Inc., the company formally organized O'Malley Drilling Investments Inc. on November 20, 2018. The business is owned and managed by Ryan O'Malley and operated as a true family enterprise.
